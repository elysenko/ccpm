# Voice AI Meeting Bot Research

## Research Question
Comprehensive analysis of real-time voice AI agent options for meeting bots with always-on listening capability (no wake word), comparing all options (self-hosted and cloud-based) with hardware requirements analysis.

## Project Structure
- `00_research_contract.md` - Scope and requirements
- `01_research_plan.md` - Query strategy and subquestions
- `02_query_log.csv` - All searches performed
- `03_source_catalog.csv` - Source quality ratings
- `04_evidence_ledger.csv` - Claims with citations
- `05_contradictions_log.md` - Conflicting information
- `08_report/` - Final deliverables
- `10_graph/` - GoT state tracking
